## 57 Artists Exhibiting Together

Exhibition, curated by the Artist Stop Being Poor
[@artiststopbeingpoor](https://www.instagram.com/artiststopbeingpoor/?hl=en) collective
and Stephan van Kuyk [@stephan.vankuyk](https://www.instagram.com/stephan.vankuyk/?hl=en#).

Stephan is a Berlin-based artist, with a career spanning over a decade. Initially starting in
street art in 2010 in Buenos Aires, he quickly starting building a network of artists and
hosting self-organizing art exhibitions. Since then, he has taken steps to take his career to
an international stage, moving to Berlin in 2015. Stephan’s work has been featured in more
than 100 exhibitions across the world. His art is part of prestigious art collections worldwide.
