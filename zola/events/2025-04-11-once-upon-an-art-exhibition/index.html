Once Upon An Art Exhibition

by Tape Art Sessions
2025-04-11

Step into a world of wonder!

On April 11th, Prachtsaal Studio in Neukölln transforms into a realm of fairytales - reimagined through the bold, immersive medium of tape art. Each piece tells a unique story, shaped by diverse cultures and perspectives.

How do you see fairytales? Come find out!

Opening hours:

Vernissage, Friday, 11.04, 18.00-22.00

Prachtsaal Studio
Jonasstraße 22
12053 Berlin

Let's create with tape! In this 3-hour workshop designed for kids aged\n6–13 (accompanied by a guardian), kids will explore the exciting\npossibilities of tape art—a fun and accessible way to create bold,\nunique designs using vibrant adhesive tapes. In this workshop, one of\nour seasoned tape artists will guide young creators through the basics\nof tape art.
\n
Kids will learn how to cut, layer, and arrange\nvibrant tapes to bring their own unique designs to life. From clean\ngeometric lines to playful curves and bold patterns, we encourage\nfollowing the imagination and experimenting.
\n
Tape Art Sessions are artist-led, artist-run workshops. We bring\nthe joy, novelty, and versatility of tape art to the hands of the people!\nRight here in Neukölln, you can learn how to create your very own tape art\npiece.  Tape art is an accessible art form – you can do it without any\nprevious experience. We provide all the materials you’ll need at our monthly\nworkshops for kids and adults!  Berlin is home to many of today’s preeminent\ntape artists. These workshops pay homage to that history and encourage folks\nof all backgrounds to give it a try! Let’s keep the artistic spirit of this\ncity alive.
