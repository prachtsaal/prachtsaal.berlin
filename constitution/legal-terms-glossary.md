## Usage Notes

1. **Legal Binding**: Per § 3(3) of the constitution, the German version is legally binding ("Only the latter is legally binding" / "Nur letztere ist rechtlich bindend").

2. **Consistency**: Always use these exact translations to maintain legal clarity and avoid ambiguity in official documents.

3. **Context Matters**: Some terms may have different translations in non-legal contexts. This glossary specifically applies to legal and constitutional documents.

4. **Updates**: When updating this glossary, ensure changes are reflected in both the English and German versions of the constitution.
