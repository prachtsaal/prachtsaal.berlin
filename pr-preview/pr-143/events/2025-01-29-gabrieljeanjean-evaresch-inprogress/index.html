In-progress

Gabriel Jeanjean and Eva Reich

2025-01-29

Gabriel Jeanjean and Eva Resch invite the audience to view their works in progress and provide valuable feedback, thoughts and input.

Gabriel is currently preparing an exhibition in Saarbrücken. It is an interactive installation, and you will be the first to beta-test it.

Eva will be doing a reading of some of the texts from her novel in progress for the first time ever. Additionally, Eva will show a short film ‘In a heartbeat’.

The programme is as follows:
18:00 Introduction to the evening and works
18:15: Reading session with questions
19:00: Installation and short film premiere
There will be time for talks and drinks between and after the various events.

Please note that the novel reading will be in German only. If you are not fluent, please arrive at 19:00 or later to avoid interrupting the reading session.
