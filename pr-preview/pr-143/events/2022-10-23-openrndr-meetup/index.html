OPENRNDR meetup #1

2022-10-23

This is our first OPENRNDR meetup in Berlin. We are a small group of enthusiasts and contributors to this creative coding framework.

We are excited to show what we have been working on, to give and receive feedback and to help each other learn.

SCHEDULE

We plan to open at 5pm and chat casually for a while. This first event is going to be small and cozy, limited to just 10 of us. A quick introduction to the framework can spontaneously take place if there's demand.

At 7pm there will be time for casual presentations. There's no need for slides. If you want to show some work (yours or by others) you can bring your laptop or some URLs and use the available computers.

There will be a projector, a 4K monitor, quad audio and more.

If you are familiar with the Creative Code Stammtisch, you have an idea of how things may roll.

Join us on Sunday and see for yourself, ask questions if you want, contribute with your comments and maybe show a project you're working on. We're looking forward to meet you all!
