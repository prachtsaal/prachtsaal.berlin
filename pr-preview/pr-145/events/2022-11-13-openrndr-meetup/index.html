OPENRNDR meetup #2

2022-11-13

This is our second OPENRNDR meetup in Berlin. We are a small group of enthusiasts and contributors to this creative coding framework. We program in Kotlin to have fun creating plotter artworks, interactive graphics with depth-cameras for theater, generative visuals with shaders and more.

We are excited to show what we have been working on, to give and receive feedback and to help each other learn.

SCHEDULE 5pm : Introduction to OPENRNDR. Bring a laptop with IntelliJ Idea Community edition installed for your OS and CPU. If you can't wait you can try to run your first programs :-) If you get stuck ask in our chats or in the forum.

At 7.30pm there will be time for casual presentations. There's no need for slides. If you want to show some of your work you can bring a laptop or some URLs and use the available computers.

There will be a projector, a 4K monitor, quad audio and more.

It's ok to join only for the introduction or the presentations.
