Artists Stop Being Poor

Art exhibition

2023-05-03

57 Artists Exhibiting Together

Exhibition, curated by the Artist Stop Being Poor @artiststopbeingpoor collective and Stephan van Kuyk @stephan.vankuyk.

Stephan is a Berlin-based artist, with a career spanning over a decade. Initially starting in street art in 2010 in Buenos Aires, he quickly starting building a network of artists and hosting self-organizing art exhibitions. Since then, he has taken steps to take his career to an international stage, moving to Berlin in 2015. Stephan’s work has been featured in more than 100 exhibitions across the world. His art is part of prestigious art collections worldwide.
